Model / Edge Logic

  • Live Hit %: baseline hit rate adjusted by current position, total score, round score, target distance, and event progress.
  • Parlay Hit %: product of every leg live hit percentage.
  • Implied %: American odds converted to break-even probability.
  • Edge: model live hit percentage minus implied hit percentage. Positive means the model likes the price.
  • EV: model probability times profit minus miss probability times wager.
  • Best use: refresh after every 3-hole stretch or at round end, then override live probabilities with DataGolf or sportsbook market probabilities when available.
